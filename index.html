About Stories De Cafe

At Stories De Cafe, we believe a great meal is more than just food — it's a moment worth remembering. Nestled at AR Gallery, Market Junction, Karunagappally our kitchen brings together the smoky richness of Arabian grills, the comfort of Indian classics, and in-house recipes crafted with care and served fresh daily.

Whether you are visiting with family, friends, or for a quick bite, we serve with care and flavor in every plate.
